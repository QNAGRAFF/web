Request By (System): Is Graffiti Illegal?

Yes graffiti is illegal to an extent (mural artist / permitted artist) more than likley have permission to do it, without any permission by the (property owner and or city) you "CAN NOT" Legally Write Graffiti!

Request By (System): Is Sticker Slapping Illegal?

Short answer is yes and no while its still illegal by law if you can take it off without leaving perminate damage then you should not worry but not only that cops dont really pay attention to stickers more so tags and throws!

Request By (System): Can i get in trouble doing graffiti?

Yes if its on (public property) if you own (x property) then you can do what ever you want to it "But" if it is owned by someone else or a city you can face up to months or even years depending on the amount of graffiti! "Note: Most of the time you will have to spend the night or day in jail and just pay some fines depending on your situation"
